Solar Services

Brizmont Energy Limited, with a legacy spanning over two decades, has positioned itself as a trailblazer in the solar services sector within Nigeria. Since our establishment in 2011, our commitment to advancing renewable energy technology has been unwavering. At the heart of our operations is a robust focus on Research and Development (R&D), coupled with expertise in manufacturing and marketing an extensive range of solar power solutions.


Headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria, Brizmont Energy Limited has cultivated a vast network of local business partners across major cities, bolstering our presence and impact. Our recent strategic realignment underscores a dedicated focus on the manufacturing and distribution of renewable energy solutions, catering to the diverse needs of domestic, industrial, and telecom applications. The Solar Warehouse business segment spearheads this initiative, ensuring a ready supply of superior solar products, including panels, inverters, battery backups, and complementary components.


In line with our ambitious goals, we aim to roll out over 100 solar mini-Grid sites through our Nayo MG utility business segment by 2021. This ambitious project reflects our commitment to providing sustainable energy solutions on a large scale. Brizmont Energy Limited's global outreach extends to the USA, Asia, and Europe, facilitating the delivery of quality products and services at competitive prices, making us a prominent player in the dynamic Nigerian market.


Brizmont Energy Limited undertakes a variety of engineering projects, ranging from revamping existing facilities to large-scale grassroots initiatives across Nigeria. Our track record reflects success in improving plant capacity, reducing energy consumption and maintenance costs, and meeting environmental standards.

With experience in designing and building innovative plants, structures, roads, and bridges, Brizmont Energy Limited has contributed significantly to Nigeria's infrastructure. We deliver world-class grassroots facilities and revamps on time and within budget, leveraging our seasoned teams' unmatched experience.

Hydro Services

Brizmont Energy Limited stands as a formidable force in the field of hydro services, boasting a comprehensive portfolio that spans consultancy, construction, and refurbishment of hydro energy projects. Our commitment to harnessing the potential of hydroelectricity is evident in our expertise in the specification and supply of self-cleaning intake screens. These screens not only enhance efficiency but also contribute to the sustainable operation of hydroelectric facilities.


Moreover, our proficiency extends to the provision of control and grid connection systems, further optimizing the performance of hydroelectric projects. Brizmont Energy Limited's dedication to environmental sustainability aligns seamlessly with our endeavors in the hydro sector, where we strive to play a pivotal role in the generation of clean, renewable power. By integrating cutting-edge technology with extensive experience, we are positioned to contribute significantly to the advancement of hydro energy solutions in Nigeria and beyond. Leveraging modern technologies and logistics methods, Brizmont Energy Limited's supply management services streamline the process efficiently. Ensuring timely and accurate material receipt, the company optimizes transportation networks, collaborates with global freight forwarders, and employs the latest supply chain technology for accurate tracking from origin to destination and installation.
