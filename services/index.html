Job Order Contracting (JOC) Services

Brizmont Energy Limited stands out as a premier provider of On-Call Construction and Job Order Contracting (JOC) services, ensuring effective and efficient project management. The company's extensive experience includes delivering Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) services to various public entities, showcasing a proven track record with a client-focused approach and the capacity to swiftly complete projects.

JOC Process Overview

  1. Determine realistic potential annual volume of job orders.
  2. Establish the contract duration, typically with a one-two year base period and multiple option years.
  3. Issue solicitation and evaluate proposals, focusing on value, length, service area, required staff, and price book.
  4. Award the contract, with Brizmont Energy Limited potentially establishing a local office for immediate project commencement.
  5. Identify repair and renovation task orders, mutually agreeing on the scope with clients.
  6. Issue a lump-sum, fixed-price job order, initiating work immediately through pre-qualified local subcontractors.
Benefits of JOC Services
  • Increases emergency response capability.
  • Reduces overall project cost.
  • Shortens project schedule.
  • Supports local small businesses.
  • Provides incentives for quality performance.
  • Offers design/build capability.
  • Minimizes disruption to internal consumers.
  • Consolidates management with one contractor.
  • Fosters a long-term relationship promoting partnership.
